Semantic Segmentation

Welcome to a new era of Computer Vision

Semantic segmentation is a computer vision task that involves classifying each pixel in an image into a category. This process allows for detailed understanding of the scene, enabling applications such as autonomous driving, medical imaging, and more.

Our Project, Our Future

This project focuses on implementing state-of-the-art semantic segmentation algorithms and evaluating their performance on benchmark datasets, such as CityScapes and GTA5.
